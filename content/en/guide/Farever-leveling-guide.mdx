<Section icon={Target} title="Core XP Mechanics: Map and Codex">

Unlike traditional MMOs where grinding mobs is the primary source of XP, Farever prioritizes discovery. To level up effectively, you must interact with the world map and complete specific region objectives.

| Leveling Source | XP Yield | Priority | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Map Objectives** | Very High | ★★★★★ | Complete gray question marks on the map. |
| **Monster Codex** | High | ★★★★☆ | Kill specific amounts of monsters (4, 8, 20). |
| **Mini-Bosses** | High | ★★★★☆ | One-time massive XP boost per boss kill. |
| **Dungeons** | Medium | ★★★☆☆ | Best for gear and runes, moderate XP. |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Professions and Survival Crafting">

Professions (Jobs) are essential for long-term progression. To unlock them, you must speak to the "Job Well-done" NPC. While you can eventually learn all of them, your gold and crafting points are limited early on.

**The Alchemy Priority**
If you are playing a class with low self-healing, such as the Rogue, Alchemy is mandatory. It allows you to craft Minor Healing Potions using vials and world-gathered materials.

| Craftable Item | Job Required | Ingredients | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Minor Heal Potion** | Alchemist | Vials, Herbs | Heals ~100 HP instantly. |
| **Skunk Stew** | Hunter/Cook | Meat, Hunter Sauce | Buffs stats for a duration. |
| **Nets** | Hunter | Gold (Merchant) | Used to catch cosmetic pets. |
| **Refined Sparks** | Smith | Salvaged Gear | Used for high-level gear upgrades. |

<Callout type="success" title="Brute Force Healing">
Healing potions are potent. Since you can walk underwater but lose HP when your air runs out, you can "brute force" underwater exploration by chugging potions to stay alive longer.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Map} title="Exploration and Fast Travel Tips">

The world of Farever is vertically designed. Knowing how to navigate the terrain will save you hours of travel time.

**Obelisk Teleportation**
Obelisks aren't just for map reveals; they are your primary fast travel network. Interact with an Obelisk to set it as your teleport point. You can "fly" between unlocked Obelisks by selecting them on the map, transforming into a ball of energy for automated travel.

**Underwater Mechanics**
There is no swimming; you walk on the seabed. 
- **Air Bar**: Watch the blue bar. When it hits zero, you take rapid damage.
- **Double Jump**: You can jump higher underwater, allowing you to reach hidden chests or quest objectives.
- **Combat**: You can fight underwater, but your movement is slightly slower.
