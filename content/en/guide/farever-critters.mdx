<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Your First Mount">

Unlocking your first mount is a priority for any new player. The world of Siagarta is massive, and walking between regions like the Valley of Eternal Autumn and Skover Island can be time-consuming. The initial mount system introduces you to the concept of mount endurance and specialized movement.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Skover Island Settlement">
    After completing the initial tutorial area, follow the main path to the first major hub on Skover Island.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Stable Master">
    Find the NPC marked with the horseshoe icon. They will provide a quest titled "A Sturdy Friend" which requires you to gather specific local herbs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete the Taming Trial">
    You will be directed to a nearby field to interact with a Wild Boar. This involves a short mini-game where you must manage your own endurance to stay mounted.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Register the Critter">
    Return to the Stable Master to receive your Mount Whistle. You can now summon your boar anywhere in the open world.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Mount Limitations">
Mounts cannot be summoned inside Dungeons or specific "Ominous" areas. Always check your mini-map for the "No Riding" icon before planning your route.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Wind} title="Aerial Mastery: Gliders and Verticality">

Siagarta is a vertical open world. You will frequently find yourself on high cliffs or floating islands where jumping off is the fastest way down. Gliders are a specific subset of **farever critters** that allow you to transition from high-altitude exploration to ground combat seamlessly.

**Glider Mechanics to Master:**
- **Airstreams**: Look for white swirling wind currents that boost your altitude.
- **Geysers**: Found in volcanic or coastal regions, these launch you high into the air.
- **Stamina Drain**: Gliding consumes stamina; if you run out mid-air, you will fall.
- **Dive Bomb**: A specialized skill for certain gliders that allows you to initiate combat from the air.
