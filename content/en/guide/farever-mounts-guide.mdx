## Claiming Your First Mount

In FarEver, you don’t need to wait until the endgame to enjoy the benefits of a mount. The developers have made it incredibly easy for new players to hit the ground running. By following a few simple steps in the shop menu, you can unlock your initial mount without spending any currency.

| Step | Action | Location |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Press **ESC** | Main Menu |
| 2 | Open **Shop** | Menu Sidebar |
| 3 | Claim Free Items | Shop "Free" Tab |
| 4 | Open **Collections** | Press **Y** |
| 5 | Select **Mount** | Bottom Tab |

Once you have claimed the items, ensure you navigate to your Collections menu by pressing 'Y'. From there, click on the Mount tab at the bottom of the screen to equip your new companion. After equipping, a dedicated mounting button will appear on your UI, which you can bind to your preferred key in the settings for maximum comfort.

> **💡 Pro Tip:** The transition between gliding and mounting is designed to be seamless. If your default keybind feels awkward, head to the Options menu and remap your mount/dismount action to a side mouse button or a key near your movement controls.

## Essential Traversal and Exploration Tips

While mounts are vital for covering long distances, FarEver features a dynamic world where exploration is rewarded with experience points and character growth. You should always keep an eye on your map for grayed-out areas that indicate unexplored objectives.

### Fast Travel via Obelisks
The world is dotted with large, glowing stones known as Obelisks. These serve as the backbone of your travel network. When you first encounter one, interacting with it allows you to set it as a respawn point. More importantly, they function as your primary fast-travel hubs.

*   **Interact:** Approach the Obelisk and select the teleportation option.
*   **Energy Travel:** You will transform into a ball of energy, soaring through the sky to your destination.
*   **Discovery:** Always prioritize finding these beacons when entering a new region to ensure you have a fallback point.

### Underwater Navigation
FarEver does not feature traditional swimming, but you can walk along the ocean floor to discover hidden chests and complete specific zone objectives. Keep a close eye on your blue oxygen/stamina bar while underwater. If it depletes, you will start taking damage, though self-healing abilities can help you survive longer in these zones.

## Combat and Utility Flexibility

Your mount is not the only thing that offers customization. Your character's combat effectiveness is split between your class skills and your weapon skills. When you find yourself in a pinch, don't forget that you can utilize your arsenal slots to equip secondary weapons that provide passive buffs or emergency healing. 

If you choose to become an Alchemist, you can craft minor healing potions using vials purchased from the guild merchant. These are essential for squishy classes that lack inherent self-sustain. Always ensure you have a few potions hotkeyed for difficult boss encounters or underwater exploration missions where you might be taking passive damage.

## FAQ

**Q: Is the mount speed upgradeable?**

A: Currently, the focus is on the base mount accessibility. As the game expands through early access in 2026, keep an eye on the roadmap for potential mount speed upgrades and new mount types.

**Q: Can I use my mount inside dungeons?**

A: Mounts are designed for open-world traversal. Dungeons in FarEver are typically designed for on-foot combat, so you will automatically dismount upon entering an instance portal.

**Q: Do I need to pay for better mounts?**

A: No. The developers have promised that the game will never be pay-to-win. All mounts and cosmetic items are earned through gameplay or claimed for free as part of the starter experience. 

**Q: How do I change my mount keybind?**

A: Navigate to the Options menu, select the Hotkeys tab, and look for the "Mount/Dismount" action. You can assign this to any key or mouse button that suits your playstyle.
