<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Mechanics and Attack Patterns">

The fight against **farever king ratsar** is divided into three distinct phases. Each phase introduces new mechanics that force players to adapt their positioning and skill rotation. Unlike standard open-world enemies, the King has a high "Poise" meter; you must land consistent heavy attacks or specific weapon skills to stagger him, opening a window for massive burst damage.

### Phase 1: The Royal Charge (100% - 70% HP)
In the initial phase, King Ratsar relies on brute force. He will frequently use a "Belly Slide" to close the distance between him and ranged players (Mages and Clerics). Warriors should stay in his face to keep aggro, while Rogues focus on flanking for backstab bonuses.

### Phase 2: The Swarm Master (70% - 30% HP)
Once he reaches 70% health, the King retreats to his throne and summons waves of elite Rat-Kin. This is the most dangerous part of the fight for solo players. You must clear the adds immediately, as they apply a poison debuff that reduces your movement speed and healing received.

| Attack Name | Type | Description | Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tail Sweep** | Physical AOE | A 360-degree spin with high knockback. | Jump or Dodge backward. |
| **Miasma Breath** | Poison / DOT | A cone-shaped cloud that lingers on the ground. | Move to the boss's flank. |
| **King's Roar** | Channeled | An ear-piercing scream that stuns all nearby players. | Use an Interrupt skill. |
| **Royal Pounce** | Gap Closer | A high-damage jump targeting the furthest player. | Side-step at the last second. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Danger Zone">
Do not stand in the green "Miasma" clouds. In Farever, environmental hazards tick for percentage-based damage, meaning even high-level players can be melted if they ignore positioning.
</Callout>

### Phase 3: The Frenzied King (30% - 0% HP)
In his final phase, King Ratsar gains a "Frenzy" buff, increasing his attack speed by 50%. He stops summoning adds but begins using his "Royal Pounce" more frequently. This is a race against time; save your ultimate abilities and high-cooldown weapon skills for this window.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Boss Walkthrough">

Follow these steps to ensure a clean victory against the rodent king. This sequence is designed for players who are attempting the dungeon for the first time in 2026.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Engagement">
    Enter the arena and let your tank (or your hardiest character) engage first. Do not use your high-damage skills immediately; wait for the boss to finish his first "Belly Slide."
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mastering the Perfect Block">
    King Ratsar's "Claw Swipe" has a very predictable wind-up. Time your block just as his hand moves forward to trigger a **Perfect Block**, which saves your endurance and builds his stagger meter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Managing the Adds">
    When the King retreats at 70% HP, move to the center of the room. This prevents the adds from flanking you. Use AOE weapon skills (like the Axe's "Whirlwind") to clear them quickly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Burn">
    When the boss enters Frenzy at 30% HP, ignore his smaller attacks and focus entirely on damage. Use your "Arsenal" skills to bypass his defenses and finish the fight before your healer runs out of mana.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Hidden Interaction">
During the "King's Roar" channel, if three players hit the boss with heavy attacks simultaneously, he will be instantly staggered for 5 seconds, regardless of his remaining Poise.
</Callout>
