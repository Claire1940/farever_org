<Section icon={Swords} title="Catching the Hidden Critter">

The main event is the capture of the **farever hidden critter**, specifically the Ladybug. This creature is small and can be easily missed if you are moving too quickly. It typically spawns near the large flower where you pick up the Xylotass Petals. Unlike combat encounters, catching a critter requires precision and the correct utility item.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip the Net">
    Open your inventory and place the Capture Net in your active utility slot. You cannot catch the ladybug with your bare hands or weapons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Spawn">
    The ladybug wanders in a small circle near Beely the Bee. It has a distinctive red shell with black spots that stands out against the green moss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Timed Capture">
    Approach the ladybug slowly. If you run, it may fly away temporarily. Use the net when the critter is within the highlighted capture zone.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Capture">
    Check your quest log to ensure the "Hidden Critter" objective has updated to "Return to Quest Giver."
  </Step>
</Steps>
