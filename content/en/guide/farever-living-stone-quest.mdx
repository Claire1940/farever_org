## Quest Prerequisites and Starting Location

Before you can officially begin the **farever living stone quest**, your character must meet specific criteria to ensure survival in the harsh mountainous terrain where the quest takes place. Most veteran players recommend reaching at least Level 45 before attempting the later stages, though skilled players may manage at lower levels with optimal gear.

To start the quest, navigate to the **Echoing Peaks** region and locate the NPC known as Elder Kaelen. He is typically found near the campfire at the entrance of the Stone-Singers’ Outpost. Speaking with him will trigger the first objective, "The Weight of History."

## Phase 1: The Whispering Canyon Navigation

The first major hurdle of the **farever living stone quest** involves navigating the Whispering Canyon. This area is shrouded in a magical fog that disorients players who move too quickly. To progress, you must follow the glowing ley lines etched into the canyon floor.

During this phase, you will encounter "Stone-Bound Sentinels." These enemies have high physical defense but are susceptible to elemental magic, particularly Wind and Water spells. Engaging them in groups is dangerous, so pulling them one by one is the safest strategy for solo players.

### Key Objectives in the Canyon:
1.  **Locate the three Resonance Crystals**: These are hidden in alcoves protected by Sentinels.
2.  **Attune the Crystals**: Use the "Chisel of Echoes" provided by Elder Kaelen on each crystal.
3.  **Activate the Great Gate**: Once all crystals are attuned, return to the canyon's end to open the path to the Living Vault.

## Phase 2: Solving the Living Stone Puzzles

Once inside the Living Vault, the **farever living stone quest** shifts from combat to logic. The "Chamber of Alignment" contains a massive floor puzzle that requires players to rotate stone pillars to match the constellations shown on the ceiling.

| Pillar Position | Required Rotation | Constellation Match |
|---|---|---|
| **North Pillar** | 90 Degrees Clockwise | The Great Bear |
| **East Pillar** | 180 Degrees | The Rising Sun |
| **South Pillar** | 270 Degrees Clockwise | The Fallen King |
| **West Pillar** | No Rotation | The Eternal Eye |

If you fail the puzzle three times, the room will fill with toxic gas, dealing periodic damage until the reset lever is pulled. It is highly recommended to study the ceiling patterns before interacting with any pillars.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not attempt to force the pillars if they seem stuck. This usually indicates an unkilled enemy is still lurking in the shadows of the room.

## Phase 3: The Stone Guardian Boss Fight

The climax of the **farever living stone quest** is the battle against Lithos, the Awakened Guardian. This boss features three distinct phases that test your positioning and timing.

### Boss Phase Overview
*   **Phase 1 (100%-70% HP):** Lithos uses slow, heavy melee attacks. He will occasionally slam the ground, creating shockwaves that must be jumped over.
*   **Phase 2 (70%-30% HP):** The boss retreats to the center and summons smaller "Pebble Thralls." You must clear these adds quickly, or Lithos will absorb them to heal.
*   **Phase 3 (30%-0% HP):** Lithos enters an Enraged state. His attack speed increases, and he begins casting "Earthquake," which deals massive area-of-effect (AoE) damage.

After defeating Lithos, the **farever living stone quest** concludes at the Altar of Ages. Interacting with the altar allows you to extract the "Heart of the Stone," which can be crafted into several different legendary items depending on your character class.

## Advanced Tips for Speedrunning

If you are looking to complete the **farever living stone quest** as quickly as possible—perhaps for a seasonal leaderboard or to gear up an alt character—consider the following optimizations:

1.  **Skip Trash Mobs**: Using invisibility potions or high-mobility skills (like Blink or Dash) allows you to bypass roughly 60% of the enemies in the Whispering Canyon.
2.  **Pre-Attune**: You can actually begin the attunement process while in combat if you have a high enough "Stagger Resistance" stat.
3.  **Burst Damage**: Save your Ultimate abilities for the transition between Phase 2 and Phase 3 of the boss fight to minimize the time Lithos spends in his Enraged state.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I repeat the Farever Living Stone Quest?**

A: While the main story version of the quest can only be completed once per character, a "Heroic" version is unlocked at Level 60, allowing players to farm the boss for upgraded versions of the rewards.

**Q: What happens if I lose the Chisel of Echoes?**

A: If you accidentally discard the Chisel of Echoes during the **farever living stone quest**, you can return to Elder Kaelen in the Echoing Peaks. He will provide a replacement for a small fee of 50 Gold.

**Q: Is the Living Stone artifact tradable with other players?**

A: No, the items crafted from the Living Stone are "Soulbound on Pickup." This means you cannot trade them or sell them on the player auction house; they are meant specifically for the character that completed the quest.

**Q: Are there any hidden achievements associated with this quest?**

A: Yes, there is a hidden achievement called "Solid as a Rock," which is granted if you defeat Lithos without taking any damage from his "Earthquake" ability in the final phase.
