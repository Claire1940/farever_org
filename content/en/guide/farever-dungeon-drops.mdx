<Section icon={Target} title="The Most Efficient Farming Routes">

Efficiency is the name of the game when hunting for **farever dungeon drops**. Running a 15-minute dungeon for the same loot you could get in a 5-minute dungeon is a waste of resources. Based on extensive community testing in 2026, certain dungeons stand out as "efficiency kings."

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gear Leveling via World Bosses">
    Before hitting dungeons, clear the world bosses in your current zone. These unrepeatable kills provide a massive initial boost to your gear score, allowing you to skip the "Green gear" phase entirely.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Azsuna Rune Loop">
    If you are low on Runes or Spark Dust, head to Azsuna. There are three sparkling patrols that move in a circular path. By the time you kill the third patrol, the first one has usually respawned. This is the fastest way to finalize your rune setup.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Spamming Lost City of Ma'ida">
    For Sea faction armor, Ma'ida is unbeatable. A well-optimized build can clear this in under 5 minutes on Hard Mode. It provides consistent Blue and Epic armor drops that scale to Level 20.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mastering Ratsar's Lair">
    For Kobold gear, avoid the buggy Ruins of Gorgon's Hollow. Ratsar's Lair is a 6-minute fight that rewards skillful play and provides the best Kobold-themed physical gear in the game.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Hard Mode Rewards">
Always run dungeons on Hard Mode once you meet the minimum requirements. The jump in loot quality from Normal to Hard is significant, shifting the drop table from mostly Green/Blue to consistent Blue/Epic.
</Callout>

While some dungeons like the Trunk of the Hive Tree are visually impressive, their mechanics (like the cannon sequence) significantly slow down your farm. Unless you specifically need a weapon from those tables, stick to the faster alternatives mentioned above.

</Section>
