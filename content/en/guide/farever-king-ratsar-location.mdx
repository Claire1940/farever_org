<Section icon={Swords} title="Boss Mechanics and Combat Strategy">

King Ratsar is an aggressive melee fighter that utilizes the environment to overwhelm players. The fight is divided into three distinct phases, each requiring different endurance management and positioning. Understanding these patterns is more important than simply having high-level gear, as the action combat in *Farever* rewards timing over raw stats.

### Attack Patterns Table

| Attack Name | Type | Description | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tail Sweep** | AOE Melee | A 360-degree spin that knocks back all nearby players | **Perfect Block** or dodge backward when he raises his tail |
| **Plague Leap** | Targeted | The boss leaps into the air and slams down on a player | Watch the red circle on the ground and **Dash** away |
| **Vermin Surge** | Summon | Ratsar retreats to a pillar and summons small rat minions | Group up and use **AOE weapon skills** to clear the adds |
| **King's Roar** | Debuff | A scream that reduces player defense for 10 seconds | Keep distance when he glows red / Use **Cleric** cleanse |
