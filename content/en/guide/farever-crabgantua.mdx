<Section icon={Target} title="Surviving Nia's Fury and the Flood">

The most complex part of the **farever crabgantua** fight is the transition into **Nia's Fury**. After the boss completes a cycle of Tidal Slashes and Devour, it will dash to the center of the arena. This signals the start of a 10-second charge time during which the entire floor becomes a death trap.

The arena will fill with water, and the only way to survive is to use the environment to your advantage. Blue circles will appear on the ground, indicating where mushroom platforms will fall. Simultaneously, water spouts will erupt, acting as temporary elevators.

| Survival Element | Function | Timing Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Water Spouts** | Launches player into the air | Do not jump too early; spouts disappear at 1 second. |
| **Mushroom Platforms** | Provides a safe standing area | Look for blue circles on the ground to predict their location. |
| **Mage Blink** | Teleports through waves | Can be used as a last resort if you miss the spout timing. |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Dash">
    Watch for Crabgantua to move to the exact center of the arena. This is the only time it uses this specific movement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate a Spout">
    Find the nearest water spout and position yourself near it, but do not stand directly on it yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The 2-Second Rule">
    Wait until the cast bar for Nia's Fury is between 2 seconds and 1.5 seconds. Jump onto the spout now to reach the maximum height.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Land on a Mushroom">
    Guide your character toward a falling mushroom platform. If you fall back to the ground before the wave passes, you will die.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Timing">
If you jump on a water spout too early, you will descend back to the ground just as the lethal wave is unleashed. Aim to be at the peak of your jump or safely on a mushroom when the timer hits zero.
</Callout>
