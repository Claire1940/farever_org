<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mechanics and the Arsenal System">

Combat in Farever is a dynamic affair that requires more than just button mashing. Whether you are playing the **farever demo** version or the full Early Access build, mastering the rhythm of "Perfect Blocks" and "Endurance Management" is the key to surviving boss encounters.

| Mechanic | Description | Player Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Perfect Block** | Timing a block exactly as an attack hits. | Negates damage without consuming endurance. |
| **Arsenal Slot** | Equipping a secondary weapon's skill. | Allows using a bow skill while wielding an axe. |
| **Endurance** | Resource consumed by dodging and blocking. | Managing this prevents being stunned or vulnerable. |
| **Skill Chaining** | Using class abilities in sequence with weapon moves. | Maximizes DPS during enemy "Stagger" windows. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Endurance Warning">
Never deplete your endurance bar entirely during a boss fight. If you are caught at zero endurance, you cannot dodge the boss's ultimate attacks, which usually results in an instant knockout.
</Callout>
