<Section icon={Zap} title="PvP and Competitive Gameplay">

Competitive players have much to look forward to. The **Farever roadmap** explicitly details a transition from a purely PvE experience to a balanced PvP environment. This includes dedicated gear that won't disrupt the PvE balance.

**Planned PvP Features:**
- **Dueling**: Challenge players in open-world hubs for practice.
- **PvP Regions**: High-risk, high-reward zones where combat is always enabled.
- **Instanced Battlegrounds**: Objective-based 10v10 or 20v20 arenas.
- **Dedicated Balancing**: Separate skill coefficients for PvP to ensure fair play.

| PvP Mode | Reward Type | Player Count |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dueling** | Bragging Rights | 1v1 |
| **Battlegrounds** | PvP Currency/Gear | 10v10+ |
| **Open World Zones** | Rare Crafting Mats | FFA / Group |
| **Ranked Arenas** | Seasonal Titles | 2v2 / 3v3 |

<Callout type="info" title="PvP Balancing">
The developers have stated that PvP gear will have specific stats that only trigger in combat against other players, preventing "power creep" in PvE dungeons.
</Callout>

</Section>
