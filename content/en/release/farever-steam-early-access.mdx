<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Arsenal and Talent Systems">

Progression in **Farever Steam Early Access** moves beyond just increasing your base stats. At level 7, the game introduces the **Arsenal System**, which is a game-changer for build diversity. This allows you to equip a secondary weapon and "borrow" one skill or passive from it to use with your primary weapon. This creates a hybrid skill bar that can cover your class's weaknesses or double down on its strengths.

Furthermore, reaching level 10 unlocks the **Talent Tree**. Though the current level cap in early access is 13, these four talent points allow you to specialize your character significantly.

| System | Unlock Level | Function | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Arsenal** | Level 7 | Secondary weapon slot | Use 1 skill from off-hand weapon |
| **Talent Tree** | Level 10 | Point-based upgrades | Add poison, crit, or speed buffs |
| **Masteries** | Random Drop | Skill variations | Changes how specific skills behave |

<Section icon={Map} title="Leveling and the Codex System">

Traditional questing exists in Farever, but it is not your primary source of experience. To level efficiently in **Farever Steam Early Access**, you must engage with the **Codex System**. This is a collection-based progression mechanic where you earn massive XP boosts by completing specific hunting goals.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fill the Codex">
    For every new mob type you encounter, you must kill 8 individuals to complete a Codex entry. This provides a significant experience chunk.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Hunt Elite Bosses">
    World bosses only need to be defeated once to complete their Codex entry. They also reveal their loot tables once finished.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate Hidden Chests">
    Chests are scattered throughout the open world. Unlike mobs, chest loot and XP are unique to your character and not shared with others.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Tower Synchronization">
    Visit towers in each region to reveal the map, unlock fast travel points, and highlight local quests.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Activity | XP Yield | Shared Loot? | Recommended Group Size |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Codex Mobs** | High (per entry) | No (XP is split) | 1-3 Players |
| **Open World Chests**| Medium | No (Personal) | Solo |
| **Instance Dungeons**| Low (Repeatable) | Yes (Pick wisely) | 3 Players |
| **Boss Instances** | High | Yes | 3 Players |

<Callout type="warning" title="Loot Warning">
In group play, loot from dungeons and world bosses is **shared**. Unlike the chests found in the open world, you will need to coordinate with your party members to decide who gets which upgrade.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Optimization and Economy">

As an indie project, **Farever Steam Early Access** currently faces some optimization hurdles. Players with mid-range PCs may experience stuttering in high-density areas or during large-scale party fights. However, the developers are actively pushing updates to improve server stability and client performance.

The economy is currently focused on gold and crafting materials. One of your first major goals should be saving **2,000 Gold** to purchase your first mount, which significantly speeds up traversal across the massive, tower-gated regions.

| Item/Service | Cost | Location | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Weapon Upgrade** | Scaling Gold/Mats | Blacksmith | Increased base damage |
| **Transmog** | Free/Materials | Wardrobe NPC | Cosmetic customization |
| **Crafting Recipes** | Variable | Skill Trainers | Create potions and gear |

<Callout type="warning" title="Performance Tip">
If you experience desync or lag in the overworld, try reducing the "Player Limit" in the settings menu. This limits the number of character models rendered simultaneously, which can help stabilize your frame rate during peak hours.
