<Section icon={ShoppingBag} title="The Farever Guild Merchant & Inventory">

While a full player-run **Farever guild** system is in development, the Guild Merchant currently serves as the most important NPC for character maintenance. This merchant is your primary source for bag upgrades, which are essential given the sheer amount of loot dropped by bosses.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Collect Gold">
    Complete map objectives and dungeons to accumulate gold. You can also right-click "Lost Packages" in your inventory to turn them into "Completed Packages" for 100 gold each at the merchant.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Purchase Pouches">
    Visit the Guild Merchant and select "Trade." Buy the Small Pouches (6 slots each) to fill your empty bag slots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Buy Utility Tools">
    The merchant also sells Vials for Alchemy and Nets for catching pets. Always keep a stack of Vials to craft healing potions on the go.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Systems: Weapons, Arsenal, and Runes">

Combat in Farever is split between your class skills and your weapon skills. Every class can equip multiple weapon types, each granting a unique set of active and passive abilities. Currently, there are over 25 weapons available, allowing for significant build diversity even within a single class.

| System | Function | How to Optimize |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Weapon Skills** | Grants 2-4 unique abilities | Level up skills with green "+" signs |
| **Arsenal Slot** | Passive skill from a second weapon | Equip a healing staff for sustain |
| **Runes** | Modifies class skill behavior | Right-click runes to unlock new traits |
| **Upgrading** | Minor stat boosts (+1 Faith, etc.) | Save materials for high-level (Lvl 20) gear |

The **Arsenal Weapon** slot is a unique mechanic that allows you to "borrow" one skill from a weapon you aren't actively holding. For example, a Rogue might equip a healing scepter in the Arsenal slot to gain a periodic shield or health regeneration.

<Callout type="success" title="Skill Synergy">
When leveling weapon skills, look for synergies. Some skills deal 50% more damage to "Slowed" enemies. Pairing this with a weapon that applies a movement debuff is the key to high DPS.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Crafting and Professions">

Professions, or "Jobs," are essential for endgame viability. While you can learn all of them, the cost of unlocking each subsequent profession increases. For beginners, **Alchemy** is the recommended starting point due to the necessity of self-healing.

Cooking is equally important but requires "Crafting Points" to produce high-value food like Skunk Stew. These points regenerate over time, so you must choose your crafts wisely.

| Profession | Key Output | Material Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Alchemy** | Healing & Mana Potions | Herbs & Vials |
| **Cooking** | Stat Buff Food | Monster Meat & Sauces |
| **Blacksmithing** | Armor & Weapon Upgrades | Salvaged Sparks & Ore |
| **Hunting** | Leather & Specialized Materials | Animal Mobs |

<Callout type="info" title="Underwater Mechanics">
Farever does not have a swimming animation; you walk on the sea floor. Use double jumps to navigate and keep an eye on your blue breath bar. You can "brute force" long underwater sections by using healing potions.
</Callout>
