<Section icon={Zap} title="How to Use the Arsenal Slot System">

The defining feature of the **Farever arsenal** is the ability to mix and match skills via Arsenal slots. This system allows you to keep the utility of one weapon while enjoying the raw power or moveset of another. If you find a weapon with an incredible ultimate ability but poor basic attack speed, you can simply place it in an Arsenal slot to extract its skill.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip Your Primary Weapon">
    Select the weapon that has the basic attack moveset you prefer. This weapon will stay in your hand during combat and determine your reach and speed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Arsenal Slots">
    As you progress through the Siagarta regions and level up your character, you will unlock additional Arsenal slots in your character menu.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assign Secondary Weapons">
    Place a secondary weapon into an Arsenal slot. You do not need to meet the full stat requirements to use its skills, though effectiveness may scale with your attributes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Map Skills to Your Bar">
    Open your skill configuration menu. You can now drag the active skills from your Arsenal-slotted weapon onto your primary hotbar, replacing your main weapon's default skills if desired.
  </Step>
</Steps>
