<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Progression for Twin Fangs">

Navigating the progression of the **farever twin fangs** requires a balance of exploration and focused crafting. You cannot simply buy the best fangs; you must gather materials from specific subregions in Siagarta and level up your Blacksmithing job.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Game: Skover Island">
    Explore the Skover region and clear small enemy camps to find "Rough Daggers." These serve as your base for the first 5 levels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Rare: Shadow's Edge">
    Once you reach Level 8, head to the Valley of Eternal Autumn. Hunt elite enemies to drop "Shadow Essence" and craft your first Rare-tier twin fangs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlocking the Arsenal">
    Complete the "Master of Arms" quest in the main hub to unlock your first Arsenal slot. Immediately slot a weapon with a mobility skill like **Dash Strike**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mid-Game: Dungeon Grinding">
    Enter the "Sunken Grotto" dungeon. The boss here drops the "Twin Fangs of the Deep," which provide a massive boost to your Evasion and Crit stats.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Late Early Access: Siagarta Peaks">
    As you push toward the current level cap, focus on the snowy peaks of Ebral. Collect "Frost-Iron" to forge the highest-tier daggers available in the 2026 roadmap.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Crafting Jobs">
Don't ignore the **Blacksmith** and **Alchemist** jobs. Blacksmithing is required to upgrade your daggers' base damage, while Alchemy provides the "Haste Potions" essential for high-end Rogue gameplay.
</Callout>
